Course Documents and Media

How to Engage with the Course Media

Throughout the course, you will explore and reflect as you watch, listen, and read media content. It will take between 1.5 to 3 hours to view and read the content weekly. You will also find that most of the media content has an introduction that lets you know why it was selected.  

Multiple elements of media in the course are embedded directly into your lessons. You will be able to identify these as they have a blue border around them. You can also always access these media sources from their original location. Click on the button above the embedded media source to see the source content. Not all media was able to be embedded. These sources may need to be opened in a new tab. Most of the media content has an introduction that lets you know why it was selected.
